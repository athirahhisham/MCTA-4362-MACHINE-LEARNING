📊

Key Predictor

Median income is the strongest single predictor of house value (correlation ≈ 0.69). Higher-income neighbourhoods sustain exponentially higher property prices.

🗺️

Location Matters

Latitude, longitude & ocean proximity are critical. Coastal Bay Area homes command 2–3× the price of equivalent inland properties in the Central Valley.

🤖

Best Model

SVR with RBF kernel outperforms all models. Its non-linear decision boundary captures complex geographic-income interactions that linear models miss.

🧹

Data Prep

207 missing values in bedrooms were median-imputed. 965 capped records ($500,001) were removed. 3 engineered ratio features were added.

⚠️

Polynomial Limitation

Polynomial regression (deg=2) was restricted to 5 features to prevent catastrophic overfitting from geographic × income interaction explosion.

⚡

SVR Trade-off

SVR was trained on 5,000 of 15,580 training samples due to O(n²–n³) complexity. Full training would likely push R² above 0.75.
